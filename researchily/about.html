Researchily was founded with a singular purpose: to bridge the gap between complex technical literature and actionable R&D breakthroughs. We believe researchers should spend their time experimenting, not skimming.

The Researchily Story

In 2024, our founders observed a critical bottleneck in the innovation pipeline. R&D teams were drowning in data, while generic AI tools were hallucinating technical details, leading to wasted laboratory budgets and delayed milestones.

We built Researchily to be the dedicated AI partner for scientists and engineers. A workspace where citations are mandatory, hallucinations are filtered out, and experiment plans are derived from the highest quality scientific literature.

Today, we support CTOs and innovation labs across Pharmaceuticals, Food & Beverage, and Materials Science, helping them turn months of research into weeks of action.
